- Web designing
- Web development
- App development
- Desktop app development

Messaging rules:

- Speak in terms of capabilities and product direction
- Do not introduce made-up project counts, client names, ratings, or delivery claims
- Keep the copy aligned with software design, interface modernization, and practical business use
