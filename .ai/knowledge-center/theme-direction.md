# Theme Direction

Reference sources:

- `.ai/resources/1.png`
- `.ai/resources/2.png`
- `.ai/resources/3.png`

Visual goals:

- Use a strong orange accent as the primary brand color.
- Add more black and charcoal to increase contrast, especially in dark mode.
- Keep light mode bright, clean, and soft with rounded surfaces and subtle depth.
- Avoid the previous purple/cyan gradient look.

Applied palette direction:

- Primary orange: `#f57420`
- Light background: warm off-white instead of cold white
- Dark background: near-black with muted warm neutrals
- Borders: soft warm gray in light mode, low-contrast charcoal in dark mode

UI direction:

- Rounded glass-like outer shells
- White content cards in light mode
- Deep charcoal panels in dark mode
- Orange glow used sparingly for emphasis only
- Header and footer should feel premium and lightweight, not heavy
